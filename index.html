About NOC

"Nobilians of Chandrapura (NOC) is an alumni association of all former students of De Nobili School, Chandrapura. NOC has been formally recognized by the school and will also work alongside JAAI (Jesuit Alumni Association of India) to accomplish various social objectives. NOC was founded in September 2015. NOC Executive committee comprises of 8 members - including the present school prinicipal as President, Mr. Dinesh Albert as Counsellor and 6 former students taking up various positions. "

Our Objective

  • Work alongside JAAI to organize health camps, educational initiatives (including financing in some cases) etc. for members of economically backward sections of the society.
  • Organize get-together of all alumni memebers in school campus once every two years (for students from All batches)
  • Coordinate with batch-wise student chapters in case gatherings are organized in school campus.
  • Publish a quarterly newsletter for alumni members on events in school, board result, achievement by Alumni members, upcoming events, NOC activities etc.
  • Organize counselling sessions by alumni for current students covering higher education, career prospects & career trends, stress management etc.
